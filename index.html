Metal Shaped by Hand, Built for Life

Iron Works is a Bali-based metalwork studio specialising in custom furniture and structural ironwork. We work directly with clients — from homeowners and villas to architects and developers — to bring raw metal to life.

Every piece starts with a conversation. We build to your exact specifications, using traditional forging techniques alongside modern fabrication. The result is work that is honest, heavy, and built to outlast trends.
